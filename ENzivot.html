Life on the department and other activity

Lužnice


Traditional annual meeting of the Plant Ecology unit is placed at Lužnice village at the end of the autumn semester.
Program: flash presentations, discussion about lectures, main presentation from different topic than plant ecology (from quantum physics to modern art), field trip and barbeque dinner.

Winter "Slunovrat" (=soltice) party


Party of the Plant Ecology unit. Party is organisated by students in the last week before Christmas.

Vegetation of Central Europe excursion


Goals of excursion is to demonstrate species diversity, vegetation types and ecological phenomena in field.

Fyt-pop-course

Course of filed methods in plant ecology, plant population biology and Phytocenology. Course is compulsory for students at the Plant Ecology unit.
